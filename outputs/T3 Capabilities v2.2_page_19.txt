Case Study: AI BOT to enhance Customer experience for a Global Retail Client Business Problem: • Global furniture store plans to enable human-like interactions and personalized customer experience across all their digital touchpoints (Kiosks, Online platforms and Mobile apps) Solution Implemented: • Chatbot was built on sophisticated NLP techniques and Generative AI models • The app sends the user request and along with the other relevant data (from the Product kb) as context in the LLM prompt • The LLM returns a succinct response to the user request based on the retrieved data • The response from the LLM is sent back to the user Tech Stack: • Llama 3 7B, Chroma DB and FAISS Business Benefits: • Client has not only elevated customer satisfaction but also achieved remarkable operational efficiencies (around ~40%) • Ensured a cohesive & consistent customer experience across channels
