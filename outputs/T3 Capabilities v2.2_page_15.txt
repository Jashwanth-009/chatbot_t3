Case Study: Automated Order Processing for a Retail Client Business Problem: • Our retail client manually processes thousands of Sales orders every day requiring huge amount of time and effort. Most of the orders are received in multiple formats like hard copies, scanned PDFs, and some from portals Solution Implemented: • Streamlined the workflow and automated the process • Sales orders from emails, handwritten scanned documents, or any other unstructured format were processed through OCR • Perform validation check and uploads the sales data into enterprise applications (ERP and CRM) Tech Stack: • FileNet Image Services, FileNet Content Manager, IBM Content Manager, OpenText Captiva InputAccel Business Benefits: • Reduction of Operational Costs by 40% • Enhanced Experience, as the data entered is almost error-free • Reduction in the cost of Claims operations
